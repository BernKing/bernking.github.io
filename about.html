About

My name is Bernardo and I am currently studying CS at Portugal. I am constantly developing software that benefits the world.

Currently solo developing Scout Vision, a platform for soccer clubs to manage and organize their scouting operations. I want to modernize the Portuguese soccer scouting experience and directly integrate AI with scouting data.

Previously, I've designed and developed other tools such as Speech Pro, a tool to get feedback on your presentation based on audio and content, which was my first web dev project in Next.js and React.

I am also interested in cybersecurity, with my first major project being Chrome Stealer, a detailed tool and walkthrough exposing the save password feature of Google Chrome. I have future projects coming up, where I will explore developing a C2 using Google Drive as the main server.
